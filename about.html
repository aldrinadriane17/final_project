About me

I'm a second year college student and aspiring visual designer. I enjoy creating clean, colorful, and user friendly designs using Figma. I'm passionate about turning simple ideas into engaging visuals and continuously improving my skills in UI, branding, and digital art.

My interest in design started when i began exploring Canva and Figma. Since then, I've been practicing layout, typography, and color theory by working on personal projects and school assignments

Outside of design, i enjoy photography, watching design tutorials, and collecting color palettes for inspiration. my goal is to become a professional UI/UX designer in the future
